Major Issues

Some common issues in training and development programs are:

High Cost-

Training programs require money for trainers, materials, and resources

Time Consuming-

Training takes time and may reduce working hours

Lack of Interest-

Employees may not show interest in training programs

Inadequate Trainers-

Lack of skilled and experienced trainers

Poor Training Design-

Training content may not match job requirements
